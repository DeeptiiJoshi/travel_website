FAQ

Frequently asked questions+

Can't find your answer? Reach out to us

01

What fitness level is required?

Our trips are designed for various fitness levels. Basic fitness is recommended as activities include hiking and outdoor adventures. We'll provide detailed information about physical requirements for each specific trip.

02

What's included in the trip price?

Trip prices include accommodation, guided activities, most meals, transportation during the trip, and necessary equipment. Airfare to the destination and personal expenses are not included.

03

What's the group size?

We keep our groups intimate, typically 8-12 people, to ensure personalized attention and a better group dynamic. This size allows for better interaction and a more authentic experience.

04

What about safety measures?

Safety is our top priority. All our guides are certified professionals with extensive experience. We provide safety briefings, quality equipment, and maintain communication with local authorities.
